Beowulf lines 1–11

Old English text

     Hwæt wē Gār-Dena      in geār-dagum
     þēod-cyninga      þrym gefrūnon,
     hū þā æðelingas      ellen fremedon!
     Oft Scyld Scēfing      sceaðena þrēatum,
     monegum mǣgðum      meodo-setla oftēah.
     Egsode eorl,      syððan ǣrest wearð
     fēa-sceaft funden:      hē þæs frōfre gebād,
     wēox under wolcnum,      weorð-myndum ðāh,
     oð þæt him ǣghwylc      þāra ymb-sittendra
     ofer hron-rāde      hȳran scolde,
     gomban gyldan:      þæt wæs gōd cyning!

Lines 1-3: Let's review some glory

Original Old English (punctuation added)

Hwæt wē Gār-Dena in geār-dagum þēod-cyninga þrym gefrūnon, hū þā æðelingas ellen fremedon!

Rearranged Old English

  1. Wē gefrūnon þrym.
  2. Wē gefrūnon þēod-cyninga þrym.
  3. Wē gefrūnon Gār-Dena þēod-cyninga þrym.
  4. Wē gefrūnon Gār-Dena in geār-dagum þēod-cyninga þrym.
  5. Hwæt wē Gār-Dena in geār-dagum þēod-cyninga þrym gefrūnon!
  6. þā æðelingas fremedon ellen.
  7. hū þā æðelingas ellen fremedon.
  8. Wē gefrūnon hū þā æðelingas ellen fremedon.
  9. Hwæt wē Gār-Dena in geār-dagum þēod-cyninga þrym gefrūnon, hū þā æðelingas ellen fremedon!

Modern English

  1. We have heard of glory.
  2. We have heard of the glory of nation-kings.
  3. We have heard of the glory of the nation-kings of the Spear-Danes.
  4. We have heard of the glory of the nation-kings of the Spear-Danes of yore-days.
  5. What we have heard of the glory of the nation-kings of the Spear-Danes of yore-days!
  6. the princes showed courage.
  7. how the princes showed courage.
  8. We have heard of how the princes showed courage.
  9. What we have heard of the glory of the nation-kings of the Spear-Danes of yore-days, how the princes showed courage!

Lines 4-5

Original Old English (punctuation added)

Oft Scyld Scēfing sceaðena þrēatum monegum mǣgðum meodo-setla oftēah.

Rearranged Old English

  1. Scyld oftēah mǣgðum setla.
  2. Scyld þrēatum oftēah mǣgðum setla.
  3. Scyld þrēatum mǣgðum setla oftēah.
  4. Scyld sceaðena þrēatum mǣgðum meodo-setla oftēah.
  5. Oft Scyld Scēfing sceaðena þrēatum monegum mǣgðum meodo-setla oftēah.

Modern English

  1. Scyld deprived tribes of seats.
  2. Scyld, with troops, deprived tribes of seats.
  3. Scyld, with troops, deprived tribes of seats.
  4. Scyld, with troops of warriors, deprived tribes of mead-benches.
  5. Often Scyld Scefing, with troops of warriors, deprived many tribes of mead-benches.
